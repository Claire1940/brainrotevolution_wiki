## What are Trinkets in Brainrot Evolution?

Trinkets represent a significant shift in the game's power scaling. Unlike pets, which can be swapped out frequently, trinkets provide a permanent "passive" layer of stats to your account. There are currently four main categories of trinkets, each tied to a specific world in the game. You can have one active trinket from each world simultaneously, meaning you can benefit from four unique boosts at once.

To obtain these items, you must use World Shards. Shards are specific to the environment they are found in; for example, you cannot use Lorilla Shards to roll for trinkets in the Goat Galaxy. 

### How to Obtain Shards
There are three primary ways to gather the shards needed for your trinket rolls:
1. **Breaking Objects:** As you move through a world and destroy environmental objects, you have a natural chance to drop shards.
2. **Trading:** The 2026 update allows players to trade shards with others. This is excellent for high-level players who have excess shards from early worlds.
3. **Robux Purchases:** For those looking to skip the grind, shards can be purchased in bundles of 500 or more.

## Brain Rot Evolution How to Equip Trinkets: Step-by-Step

Equipping trinkets in Brainrot Evolution is slightly different from equipping pets or relics because the system is designed to be largely automatic and stat-based. You do not manually "slot" them into a hotbar. Instead, you manage them through your character's internal statistics menu.

**Follow these steps to manage and check your equipped trinkets:**

1. **Open the Settings Menu:** Look for the gear icon on the left or bottom of your screen.
2. **Navigate to Stats:** Inside the settings, locate the "Stats" tab.
3. **View Active Trinkets:** Here, you will see a list of your current passive bonuses. The game automatically applies the highest-tier trinket you have unlocked for that specific world.
4. **Auto-Roll Management:** When using a trinket machine, you can toggle "Auto Roll." The machine will continue to spend shards until you manually stop it or run out of currency.

> **💡 Tip:** If you find a rarer trinket (like a Gold or Red tier) while your auto-roll is active, the game will automatically update your passive stats to reflect the better boost.

## FAQ

**Q: Do trinkets disappear when I rebirth in Brainrot Evolution?**

A: No. One of the best features of the trinket system is that they are permanent account upgrades. Once you unlock a trinket, its boosts stay with you through every rebirth, making subsequent leveling much faster.

**Q: Can I equip more than one trinket from the same world?**

A: No, you can only have one active trinket per world. If you roll a new trinket from the same world, the game will automatically use the one with the better stats. This is why the **brain rot evolution how to equip trinkets** process is considered passive.

**Q: What is the rarest trinket in the game?**

A: The Rainbow rarity trinkets are the rarest, with a 0.05% drop rate. These are currently found in the Goat Galaxy machine and provide the highest XP and damage multipliers available in 2026.

**Q: How do I get shards without spending Robux?**

A: You can earn shards by breaking objects in the world associated with those shards. Additionally, you can trade with other players or use promotional codes like "trinket" to get a head start on your collection.
