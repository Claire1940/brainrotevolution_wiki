## Brain Rot Evolution How to Rebirth: Step-by-Step

To progress through the game efficiently, you need to know exactly where to go once you hit your level milestones. Below is the detailed path for the first eight rebirths, specifically focusing on the Brain Heaven expansion which contains some of the most elusive secrets.

### Rebirth 1 to 3: The Hidden Objects
The initial rebirths are designed to teach you to look beyond the obvious. For Rebirth 1, you must search the perimeter of the map. There is a specific bush located near the starting zone of the world; walking directly into the center of this bush will trigger the rebirth prompt.

For Rebirth 2, the location shifts slightly. You will need to find a small, inconspicuous spot on the ground that doesn't look like a button but functions as one. Rebirth 3 is where things get "rot-heavy." You must find the NPC known as **Torture Turt**. ### Rebirth 4: The Code Entry
Unlike the physical triggers, Rebirth 4 requires a manual entry. Many players get stuck here looking for a button that doesn't exist. To unlock this tier, you must open the code entry menu (or sometimes the chat, depending on the current 2026 patch) and enter "rebirth 4" to verify your progress.

### Rebirth 5 to 7: Interactions and Sacrifices
Rebirth 5 involves the **Mr. Toaster** NPC. In some versions of the game, this is tied to a "Subscribe" prompt where you must interact with the NPC's dialogue to trigger the reset. 

Rebirth 6 is tied to your economy. You need to have accumulated enough wins to open the high-tier eggs. The rebirth trigger is actually inside one of these eggs, meaning you have to gamble your wins to find the path forward.

Rebirth 7 is perhaps the most unique. To achieve this, you must "sacrifice" yourself. This involves unequipping your powerful pets and allowing a high-level enemy, such as the **Troller Trala** or the **Shark**, to defeat you. Upon death, if you meet the level requirement, you will respawn with your seventh rebirth completed.

## FAQ

**Q: Why can't I find the rebirth button in Brain Heaven?**

A: In Brain Heaven, the rebirth buttons are intentionally hidden to encourage exploration. Check inside the bushes near the spawn or interact with NPCs like the Torture Turt. If you are on Rebirth 4, remember that it is a code, not a physical button.

**Q: Does rebirthing remove my pets?**

A: No, rebirthing only resets your character level and your current strength. Your pets, wins, and unlocked worlds remain intact, allowing you to level back up much faster than the first time.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get to level 109 for Rebirth 8?**

A: The fastest method is to equip a "Secret" tier pet and hit the **Squallo Cavalo** or the **Pillar** in the final world. This usually provides enough XP to hit the requirement in just a few hits.

**Q: Are there any active codes for rebirths in 2026?**
