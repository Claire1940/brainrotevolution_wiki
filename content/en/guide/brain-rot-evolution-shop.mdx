## FAQ

**Q: What is the best item to buy first in the brain rot evolution shop?**

A: You should always prioritize **Extra Relic Slots**. Relics provide the largest XP and Speed multipliers in the game, which are essential for speeding up the rebirth process.

**Q: How do I get more Index Points for the shop?**

A: Index Points are earned by discovering new Brain Rots and Pets. Every time you "unlock" a new entry in your collection book (the Index), you are rewarded with points that can be spent on permanent upgrades.

**Q: Are the YouTuber relics like the Luke Helmet worth the gems?**

A: Yes. YouTuber-tier relics often have stats that exceed standard Legendary items. The Luke Helmet, for instance, provides a massive XP boost that is difficult to match with common gear found in early-game crates.

**Q: Can I trade items bought from the shop with other players?**

A: Most items from the **brain rot evolution shop**, such as pets and basic relics, are tradable. However, certain permanent upgrades bought with Index Points are bound to your account and cannot be transferred.
