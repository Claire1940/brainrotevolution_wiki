## FAQ

**Q: What is the best secret for beginners in Brainrot Evolution?**

A: The **Bop Bop Bop Bop Sahor** is highly recommended. It spawns in multiple worlds and has a relatively low pity luck threshold, making it the most reliable first secret to farm.

**Q: Can I trade fused secrets with other players?**

A: Yes, most fused secrets like the **Garagore** or **Da Da Da** are tradable. However, limited-time event pass secrets (like the Prestige Pee Bye) are typically locked to your account.

**Q: How do I find the Fusion Forge?**

A: The primary Fusion Forge is located on the right side of **Unin Olympus**. More advanced "Restaurant Fusions" can be found in the **Kadabra Restaurant** area.

**Q: Is there a way to guarantee a secret drop?**

A: Some crates feature a "pity luck" system. For example, the **Saturnas** secret is guaranteed after opening 500 crates. Always check this **brain rot evolution guide** or the in-game crate menu to see if a pity timer is active.
