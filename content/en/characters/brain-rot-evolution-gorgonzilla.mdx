## The Los Alecios Secret Event

The "Los Alecios" weather event is a rare occurrence with a reported 1 in 175 chance of spawning. During this event, the sky transforms, and special crates spawn across all worlds. To maximize your chances of finding the Los Alejos secret relic, you must farm specific locations where item density is highest.

1.  **Monitor the Timer:** The event typically lasts only 5 minutes.
2.  **Head to the Nuclear Core:** This is the "goated" spot for crate farming because items spawn in tight clusters.
3.  **Use Private Servers:** In public servers, players often fight over crates. A private server ensures you collect all 50+ potential drops per event.
4.  **Open in Bulk:** The secret "Soccer Ball" relic has a 0.5% drop rate. Saving at least 100 crates before a mass opening is recommended for better RNG.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Los Alejos secret relic provides a 370% boost, but many players find the "Soccer Ball" variant to be "mid-tier" compared to the high-level Admin relics. Don't burn all your Robux on these crates unless you are a completionist.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the Gorgonzilla evolution?**

A: You must reach the current max level of 98. This is best achieved in the Cadabra Restaurant world using a combination of the Ultra Flame trinket and Triple XP potions.

**Q: Is the Kiwi Benedito better than the Gorgonzilla?**

A: No. While the Kiwi Benedito has decent damage, its attack range is very poor. The Gorgonzilla (level 98) has superior range, damage, and visual effects, making it the best character currently in the game.

**Q: What does the "La Vaca Goat" rebirth do?**

A: This is the seventh rebirth. It grants a massive XP multiplier and allows you to start your next run at level 82 instead of level 1, significantly speeding up the process of returning to the **brain rot evolution gorgonzilla** stage.

**Q: Where is the best place to farm crates during weather events?**

A: The Nuclear Core is widely considered the best farming spot due to the close proximity of enemy spawns and item drop locations. It allows you to collect crates much faster than the sprawling maps like Glorbo Heights or the Brain Rot Abyss.
