## Advanced Mechanics: Trinkets and Relic Smelting

To optimize your damage output for the late-game worlds like Goat Galaxy, you must master the Trinket system. Trinkets provide passive multipliers to your experience gain and attack power.

### Trinket Tiers and Effects

1.  **Common (Gray):** Provides a +5% Experience boost. Useful for early leveling but should be replaced quickly.
2.  **Rare (Green/Blue):** Includes the Scarab and Hermes trinkets. These offer a balance of speed and power.
3.  **Epic (Purple):** Hard to obtain through the Lottery Lot, but essential for mid-game progression.
