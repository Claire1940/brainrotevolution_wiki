## Scaling Abilities and Secret Milestones

The true power of the **brain rot evolution last brainrot** lies in its passive abilities. These are unlocked based on the number of unique secrets you have discovered throughout your gameplay history. This mechanic encourages players to revisit older worlds and open crates they might have skipped.

1. **25 Secrets Milestone:** You gain a 15% chance to deal **2x Damage** on every hit.
2. **50 Secrets Milestone:** You gain a 15% chance to deal **2x Range**, allowing the unit to strike enemies from across the map.
3. **100 Secrets Milestone:** This is the ultimate goal. You gain a 15% chance to deal **3.5x Damage AND 3.5x Range** simultaneously.

When these procs happen at the 100-secret level, the La Everything Combinazion can one-shot bosses like the Squallo Cavalo in Brain Heaven. Players with over 1,200 secret boosts will find that this unit outclasses even legendary admin units like the Luke Blocks character.
