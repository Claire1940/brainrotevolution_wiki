## What is the Squalo Cavallo?

The Squalo Cavallo is a secret evolution that translates from Italian as "Shark Horse." It features the upper head and jaws of a predatory shark mounted on the muscular, galloping body of a horse. This hybrid nature gives it a distinct advantage in the *Brainrot Evolution* ecosystem, allowing it to feel at home in diverse environments.

The character is famous for its unique audio cue—shouting its own name—and its lack of a traditional neck, which adds to its "goofy" aesthetic. Despite the humorous appearance, its bite attack is remarkably efficient for clearing mobs quickly.

## Advanced Strategy: The Cadaver Restaurant

The Cadaver Restaurant is the pinnacle of the current **brain rot evolution squalo cavallo** journey. This world is styled like a high-end saloon and features some of the most bizarre enemies in the game.

### Enemies and Consumables
In this world, you are no longer eating trash or basic animals. You are consuming "gourmet" brain rot.
*   **Sushi & Fish:** Ideal for the shark half of your evolution.

### Rebirth Mechanics
Once you hit level 100 in the Cadaver Restaurant, you have the option to Rebirth. In 2026, the rebirth system is more forgiving. Instead of resetting to level 1, a Rebirth 7 player might be reset to level 82 in the **Lavaca Gola** world.
