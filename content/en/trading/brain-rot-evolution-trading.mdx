## Advanced Trading Strategies

Once you have established a decent inventory, you can start "flexing" your rarer items to see what max-level players are willing to offer. Look for players around level 100+; they often have an abundance of secrets and gems but may be missing specific event items or crates.

1. **The Sweetener Method**: If a trade is close to being accepted but the other player is hesitating, add a low-value "sweetener" like a Vamp Duck or a basic Giftini.
2. **Server Hopping**: If a lobby is "dry" or filled with low-level players, don't waste time. Hop to a new server to find a fresh pool of potential trading partners.
3. **Crate Bundling**: Instead of trading one crate at a time, offer 3-4 Meow Crates at once. This often triggers "overpay" responses from wealthy players who want to do a bulk opening.
4. **The "Nothing to Everything" Mindset**: Always look to trade your current best item for two items of slightly lower value that combined are worth more. Repeat this until you have a massive inventory.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be wary of trades that seem too good to be true. Always double-check the item names in the trade window, as some common items have similar icons to rare secrets.
