## How to Calculate Trade Fairness

Trading in Brainrot Evolution isn't just about matching numbers; it's about understanding liquidity. A Meowl is "liquid," meaning you can sell it almost instantly for its full value. A 2-star Bandito, while technically worth 550,000, is much harder to sell because fewer players are looking for mid-tier evolved pets.

1.  **Check the Star Rating:** Never trade a 2-star pet for a base pet of a higher rarity without significant "adds" (extra items).
2.  **Evaluate Damage Output:** If a trade increases your total team damage by more than 20%, it is generally considered a win, even if the coin value is slightly lower.
3.  **Verify Secret Status:** Secrets like Meowl have a fixed drop rate that makes them immune to the typical inflation seen with Epic and Legendary units.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be cautious of players offering "untradeable" items like certain 3-Tone Charms. Always verify if an item can actually be placed in the trade window before agreeing to a deal.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the most valuable item in Brainrot Evolution in 2026?**

A: You need a total of nine base pets. First, combine three base pets to make a 1-star pet.
