## Sun Coins and the Roll for Rewards System

A major mechanical addition in this update is the Sun Coin system. Unlike standard wins or trophies, Sun Coins are collected by interacting with sun orbs scattered around the world or dropped by high-level mobs. These coins are spent at the "Roll for Rewards" statue located in the center of Brain Heaven.

The rewards from this statue are essential for late-game optimization. This is where you can unlock additional pet slots and speed boosts, though the costs are "diabolical," reaching into the trillions for high-end upgrades. Most importantly, the statue is the only way to obtain specific secret brain rots that cannot be found through standard leveling.

| Reward | Cost / Chance | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Pet Slot** | 100 Trillion Sun Coins | Increases active pet capacity |
| **Speed Boost** | 7.5 Trillion Sun Coins | Permanent movement speed increase |
| **Malinino** | 1% Roll Chance | High-tier secret brain rot |
| **Tree Tree Sore** | 0.1% Roll Chance | 180% Strength multiplier |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not spend all your Sun Coins on pet slots early. Save enough to roll for the Tree Tree Sore secret, as its strength multiplier is vital for defeating the world boss.

This rebirth is essentially mandatory if you plan on competing in the global leaderboards or reaching the absolute maximum levels of the Sentinel form.
