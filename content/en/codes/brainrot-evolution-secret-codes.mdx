## Dungeon and Arena Secrets

If you prefer a guaranteed path over RNG weather, dungeons are your best bet. These require high levels and specific combat strategies.

1. **Vault of Jupiter (Lilla Desert):** Reach the far right of the desert to find this dungeon. Defeating the boss grants Vault Crates with a 1% chance for the **Elbazante**.
2. **House of Echoes (Chocola Romania):** Located on the left side, this dungeon provides Lime Crates. You have a 1% chance to pull the **Pot Hotspot**, which is vital for early-game progression.
3. **Grand Arena (Glorbo Heights):** This is currently the final dungeon secret. You must be at least level 57 to enter. It rewards Guardian Crates containing the **Bini Guardini**.

## The Fusion Forge: 2026 Recipes

Fusions are where the true power lies. To use the Fusion Forge, you must travel to Unin Olympus or the Kadabra Restaurant. These units cannot be found in crates and require you to sacrifice multiple secrets you have already collected.

### Top Fusion Recipes

*   **Garore:** Requires Elbazante + Spaghetti Toileti + Chacha Gi. This unit is widely considered a top 10 secret due to its incredible range and lack of a voice line.
*   **Haka Horage:** One of the most expensive units in the game. You must sacrifice a Garore, a Tic Tacore, and a Job Job Sahor. 
*   **Blueberini Tatachini:** A recent addition that requires Blue Octoini + Guardini + Malamea Mallay. It features unique special effects and high base power.
*   **Strawberry Octoini:** A powerful offensive unit created by combining a Blueini Octoini and a Grapefruit.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Fusing units is permanent. Once you sacrifice the ingredients, they are gone from your inventory forever. Make sure you have duplicates if you plan on keeping the original units for your collection.
