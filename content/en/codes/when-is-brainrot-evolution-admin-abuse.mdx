## Strategy: Maximizing Your Gains as a Noob

If you are starting a fresh account during an Admin Abuse event, your priority should be speed and hitbox management. Experts suggest the following steps to ensure you don't walk away empty-handed:

### 1. The Hitbox Advantage
When crates are spawning in massive clusters, using a pet with a large hitbox is essential. Pets like the **Liry Liry** or similar wide-reaching units allow you to break multiple crates or items simultaneously. This is especially vital during the Supermarketino phase, where you must collect 40 items quickly to secure the best rewards.

### 2. Potion Management
Before the event starts, ensure you have a **Triple XP Potion** and a **Luck Potion** active. Admins often give out codes or free items at the start of the event; use these immediately to boost your level so you can access higher worlds where crates might be less contested.

### 3. Rebirthing Tactics
A common mistake is rebirthing right as the event starts. While rebirthing increases your long-term multiplier, it resets your level to one, making you significantly slower. 
> **💡 Tip:** Only rebirth during an Admin Abuse event if you have already secured a high-damage pet (1B+ damage) that can carry you back through the early levels instantly.

## Advanced Event Bosses: Supermarketino and Medus

The highlight of any Admin Abuse session in 2026 is the appearance of the event bosses. These aren't standard enemies but interactive world events.

**The Supermarketino Challenge:**
When the Supermarketino appears, he requires players to bring him "groceries" scattered around the map. You should aim for a "Full Bag" (40 items) before returning to him. Bringing a full bag significantly increases the quality of the Supermarket Crates you receive. If you return with fewer than 40 items, the rewards are drastically diminished.

**The Medus Hunt:**
Medus is a roaming boss that spawns specialized Medus Crates as he is damaged. Unlike standard crates, these often despawn quickly. To farm Medus effectively, follow him into the **Trolle Ocean** or **Lorilla Desert**. If you are under-leveled, focus on the smaller crates he leaves in his wake rather than trying to solo the boss itself.
