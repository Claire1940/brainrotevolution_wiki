## FAQ

**Q: What is the easiest brain rot evolution secret to get for beginners?**

A: The **Bop Bop Bop Bop Sor** is the most beginner-friendly secret. You can find the event boss in almost every world starting from the Lilla Desert. The crates have a high drop rate and a generous pity system.

**Q: Can I still get the Bananaito Bandito in 2026?**

A: No, the Bananaito Bandito is a discontinued exclusive pack secret. It can only be acquired if you find a player willing to trade a legacy account or if the developers re-release it in a future "Throwback" event.

**Q: How do I access the Fusion Forge?**

A: The Fusion Forge is located in the **Unin Olympus** zone. You must reach the required level to enter the area, at which point you can interact with the Forge to view all available recipes and requirements.

**Q: Which secret has the highest damage output?**

A: Currently, the **I2 Perfectini Fox** holds the title for the highest potential DPS due to its 210% base boost and its unique ability to land five rapid-fire attacks simultaneously.
