## The Kadabra Restaurant: Hakahorage and Tic Tac Sahor

The Kadabra Restaurant introduces a different set of **brain rot evolution fusion secrets** that move away from the Forge and toward the Chef NPC. The star of this area is the Hakahorage, a unit known for its "uncooked noodle" limbs and massive 75 OC base damage.

To get the Hakahorage, you first need to master the Tic Tac Sahor recipe. This requires a sub-unit called "Job Job Sahor," which is arguably one of the most tedious units to farm in the 2026 update.

### How to Craft Job Job Sahor
You will need to open a variety of crates to find the following four components:
1.  **Cheese Crocodile**
2.  **Ring Ring Bus**
3.  **Pulu Pulu Pulp Pin** (Warning: This has a very low 0.5% drop rate).
4.  **Job Job Sahor Base**

Once Job Job Sahor is assembled, you can combine it with a "Guard Good Guard" to finally unlock the Hakahorage. This unit provides a 225% strength buff over your previous best brain rot, making it a top-tier choice for clearing the final worlds.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Pulu Pulu Pulp Pin is notorious for "scamming" players. Be prepared to open over 500 crates to guarantee a drop.
