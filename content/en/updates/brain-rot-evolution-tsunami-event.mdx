## Mastering the Brain Rot Evolution Tsunami Event

The core of this update is the Tsunami Game Mode, which is accessible through a purple portal located in the first world. Unlike standard gameplay, this mode functions as an "escape" challenge. Players must navigate a linear path, avoiding the rising tide while collecting crates and upgrading their base stats.

To succeed, you must focus on two primary upgrades at your event base: **Speed** and **Carry Capacity**. Speed allows you to outrun the tsunami and reach the further reaches of the map, while Carry Capacity determines how many event crates you can bring back to your base at once.

| Upgrade Type | Primary Benefit | Strategic Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Speed** | Increases movement velocity to outrun the water. | **High** - Essential for reaching the Cosmic Region. |
| **Carry Capacity** | Allows you to hold more crates simultaneously. | **Medium** - Important for efficient farming. |
| **Base Floors** | Unlocks additional space for placing crates. | **Low** - Only necessary once your inventory is full. |

> **💡 Tip:** Prioritize Speed upgrades until you can comfortably reach the end of the map. The rarest crates, such as the King and Ship crates, are located at the very back of the course.

## New Secret Units and Stats

The **brain rot evolution tsunami event** introduces five new secrets, each with a 0.5% drop rate from their respective crates. These units are designed to be "meta-breakers," with many of them offering power boosts of over 180% compared to your strongest existing brain rot.

### 1. Trolero Kingala
Tied for the strongest unit in the update, the Trolero Kingala is a massive power spike for any player. It features a distinct Dracula-inspired aesthetic but with significantly enhanced combat animations. It provides a 185% strength boost, making it a must-have for endgame progression.

### 2. Tok Tac Tok Talk
Also sitting at a 185% strength boost, this unit is visually similar to the Tok Tok Sahor but with a unique "glitch" aesthetic. It is currently one of the most sought-after units due to its high DPS and rare attack patterns.

### 3. Yanini Doctorini
While slightly less powerful than the Kingala at 180% strength, the Doctorini is a fan favorite due to its Wolverine-style slash attacks. It is often considered more visually impressive than the Worm Rot and serves as a top-tier mid-to-late event unit.

### 4. Ship Ship Ship Sahor
This pirate-themed unit offers a 182% strength boost. Its unique model allows players to essentially "walk around" as a pirate ship. While its attack speed is slightly slower than the Tok Tac Tok, its area-of-effect (AoE) damage is formidable.

### 5. Worm Rot
The Worm Rot is the most "common" of the secrets, though it still maintains a 0.5% drop rate. It offers a 175% strength boost. While it is smaller than many expected, its consistency in combat makes it a reliable addition to your secret boost total.

## How to Farm Crates Efficiently

To maximize your gains during the brain rot evolution tsunami event, you should follow a specific farming loop. The event is periodic, meaning the portal is not always open. When the "Admin Event" or "Weather Event" begins, you must act quickly.

1.  **Enter the Portal:** Check the timer in World One. Portals usually open every 30 to 60 minutes.
2.  **Redeem the Code:** Use the code **"tsunami"** immediately to receive Triple XP potions, which will help you level up your new secrets faster.
3.  **The Speed Run:** Ignore the early crates (Worm and Doctor) and use your speed to reach the back of the map. The King and Ship crates are located in the "Cosmic Region" and provide the highest value.
4.  **Open at Base:** You must open the crates within the event world to keep the items. Once a secret is unlocked, it will appear in your main game index.

The integration of the [Roblox engine's physics](https://www.roblox.com) in the tsunami mode means that movement is more fluid than the standard base-building mode. Use this to your advantage by jumping over obstacles to maintain momentum.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the tsunami event progress stay after the portal closes?**

A: Yes, any secrets or relics you unlock and "equip" to your index will remain on your account permanently. However, you can only farm new crates while the portal is active in World One.

**Q: What is the best secret to aim for in the brain rot evolution tsunami event?**

A: The Trolero Kingala and Tok Tac Tok Talk are currently tied for the highest power boost at 185%. If you are looking for pure damage, these are your primary targets.

**Q: How do I fix the "invisible crate" glitch?**

A: This is a common server-side bug in the 2026 update. If your crates appear as sparks, you can still click them to open them. Relogging or switching servers can sometimes restore the 3D models.

**Q: Can I trade the tsunami event secrets?**

A: Currently, secrets unlocked via event crates are bound to your account index. However, developers have hinted that a future update may allow for crate trading between players in the base world.
