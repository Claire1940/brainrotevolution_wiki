## Unlocking the La Everything Combinazion

The La Everything Combinazion is the most complex fusion ever released in the history of the game. Unlike previous fusions that required three or four basic units, this God-tier entity requires seven specific secrets and sub-fusions. To begin this process, you must travel to World 6 and locate the fusion area to the left of the main spawn.

### Required Fusion Components
To successfully craft the La Everything, you must possess the following seven units in your inventory:

1.  **Graus Medus:** A classic secret that remains relevant for this high-tier recipe.
2.  **Blue Birini Octoini:** Often referred to as the "Blueberry" secret, this unit requires significant crate opening luck.
3.  **Pee Pee Kiwi:** A secret with a **1% drop rate**. While more common than others, it is essential for the "Everything" theme.
4.  **Job Job Sahor:** This is a sub-fusion. You must first combine a Crocodilo, a Ring Rang Buzz, and a Pulen to create this ingredient.
5.  **Man Man:** One of the rarest components with a **0.5% drop rate**. This unit typically requires hitting the "pity" counter on crates for most players.
6.  **Grapefruiti:** A 1% drop rate secret that is relatively easy to obtain compared to the Man Man.
7.  **Unendan Dendan:** Another sub-fusion located in the Unend Olympus area. You will need a Nightmare Yoni (Yoni Canalone), a Lossios, and a Berini to craft it.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to fuse these units until you have confirmed you have duplicates of your favorites. The fusion process consumes all seven units permanently to create the La Everything Combinazion.

## Scaling Abilities and Secret Boosts

What sets the La Everything unit apart from every other unit in the **brain rot evolution update** is its unique ability to grow stronger based on your account's history. The unit checks your total "Secret Boost" count—the number of unique secrets you have unlocked or currently hold—to activate passive buffs.

### Ability Thresholds
| Secret Count | Ability Effect | Proc Chance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **25 Secrets** | 2.0x Damage Multiplier | 15% |
| **50 Secrets** | 2.0x Range Increase | 15% |
| **100+ Secrets** | **3.5x Damage & 3.5x Range** | **15%** |

If you have a high secret count (some players report boosts as high as 1,270), the La Everything unit becomes an unstoppable force. The 3.5x damage and range proc creates a "cosmic explosion" effect that can cover nearly the entire screen, making it the most efficient farming tool for the Squallo Cavalo boss.

## The GGG Sahor: A New Secret Powerhouse

While the La Everything is the star of the update, the **GGG Sahor** is a formidable unit in its own right. Found in the new GGG Sahore Crate, this unit has a **0.5% drop rate**. It is essentially a visual and statistical upgrade of the Blender and Cappuccino Ascino units.

### GGG Sahor Stats at a Glance
*   **Drop Chance:** 0.5%
*   **Base Power:** ~182 (Pre-multipliers)
*   **Visual Style:** High-speed blender aesthetic with neon effects.
*   **Utility:** Excellent mid-game unit for those who haven't yet gathered the seven secrets for the God fusion.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are struggling to pull the GGG Sahor, ensure you are using Luck Potions. You can obtain these by using the new active code: **common**. While the code name sounds basic, it provides essential XP and Luck boosts for crate opening.

## Farming Strategy for World 6 (Chiclet Romania)

To maximize your efficiency in the latest **brain rot evolution update**, you should focus your efforts on World 6. The "Brain Heaven" zone is currently the best place to test the limits of your new God-tier units. 

1.  **Equip for Range:** Because the La Everything unit has massive range scaling, equip accessories that further enhance AOE.
2.  **Pity System Utilization:** When hunting for the Man Man or GGG Sahor, stay in the same server if possible. Some players believe the pity counter is more reliable during single sessions.
3.  **XP Potion Timing:** Only use the XP potions from the "common" code when you are about to do a massive fusion, as the level of your components can sometimes influence the starting level of the resulting unit.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the fusion altar for the La Everything unit?**

A: The fusion altar is located in World 6 (Chiclet Romania). Look to the left of the spawn area to find the "La Everything" interaction point.

**Q: Does the brain rot evolution update include any new codes?**

A: Yes, you can use the code **common** to receive XP potions and luck boosts. These are very helpful when trying to pull the 0.5% rarity secrets required for the new fusions.

**Q: How many secrets do I need to max out the La Everything's abilities?**

A: You need at least 100 secrets to unlock the maximum boost, which provides a 15% chance to deal 3.5x damage and 3.5x range.

**Q: Is the GGG Sahor better than the La Everything unit?**

A: No. While the GGG Sahor is a very strong secret unit, the La Everything is a God-tier rarity unit that requires the GGG Sahor (or its components) and several other secrets to create. It is significantly more powerful.
