## Mastering the Brain Rot Evolution Void Event: New Worlds and Bosses

The Void Part 2 update introduces the Void-rassic World (sometimes referred to in patch notes as Void Rot City). This area is significantly more difficult than previous zones, requiring players to be at least level 9 to enter. Once inside, the primary goal is to break nests and defeat raptors to gain the XP necessary for the next evolution tier.

The most notable addition is the **Hydrotops World Boss**. This prehistoric titan has millions of health points and can easily wipe out players who haven't optimized their defensive stats. To successfully farm this boss, you must balance your damage-per-second (DPS) with health regeneration, as the battle is often a war of attrition.

## Best Pets and Relics for the Void Realm

In Brain Rot Evolution, your base stats are only half the battle. The pets you equip act as multipliers for your damage. In the Void realm, specific pets like the **Void Mage** and **Aurora** are essential because they provide flat bonuses to damage within the Void zone.

### Recommended Loadout
1.  **Relic 1: Golden Staff** — Provides a massive base damage multiplier.
2.  **Relic 2: Void Mage** — Specifically tuned for +10 damage in the Void realm.
3.  **Relic 3: Elite Cape** — Increases movement speed, allowing you to move between nests faster.
4.  **Pet Slot: 2-Star Aurora** — The 2% drop from the new Void Egg. Merging these into 2-star versions is vital for endgame DPS.

| Pet Name | Rarity | Base Bonus | 2-Star Bonus |

## Advanced Farming Strategy

To reach level 16 in a single session, you must optimize your "Wins" per hour. Wins are the currency used to buy more pet slots and eggs. High-level players should focus on the following loop:
1.  Equip your best "Wins" multiplier pets.
2.  Farm the Hydrotops boss until you have 200 million+ wins.
3.  Spend all wins on the 2% Aurora Egg.
4.  Merge all pets to 2-star versions.
5.  Switch to your "Damage" loadout and focus on high-XP Dino Vines.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I enter the Void-rassic World in the brain rot evolution void event?**

A: You must reach level 9 in the previous world. Once you hit level 9, a portal or "Escape" button will become available, allowing you to transition into the new event area.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get the Trippy Tropa?**

A: The fastest way is to stack XP potions and focus on the Hydrotops boss once you can deal at least 90,000 damage per hit. If your damage is lower, stick to breaking nests in the desert area of the Void realm.

**Q: Is the "Secret" brain rot better than the Trippy Tropa?**

A: Currently, the Secret brain rot (Nuclear Dinosaur) has higher health, which is great for tanking bosses, but the Trippy Tropa (Level 16) has superior damage output and attack speed for general farming.

**Q: What does the code "void part two" give you?**

A: It provides a free spin on the Void Wheel. This wheel contains rare items like the Void Staff, which is one of the best relics for increasing your damage during the event.
